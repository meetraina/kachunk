North star

The week you can hold.

Kachunk replaces the calendar its people can't keep with weekly buckets they fill with toy-like blocks — one block per done thing. For ADHD adults, visual thinkers, and default parents whose priorities reshuffle faster than a calendar can. One notification. Zero streak shame. Pay once.

Risks & blockers

HIGH RISK Name is screened, not cleared. The KACHUNK screen found no collisions, but no USPTO/TESS search or counsel review has run. Do not spend on domains/handles/store listings until clearance. Unblocks: TESS knockout + WHOIS, ~1 hr + optional counsel.

MEDIUM iOS notification limits. Web-push on iOS requires the installed (Add-to-Home-Screen) app and stays best-effort. Mitigated in copy + the Roundup dot; fully solved by the store wrapper.

MEDIUM Google sign-in is a stub. Drive appData sync needs an OAuth client (free, ~1 day inc. review friction). Architecture already assumes it (D-06).

LOW Public repo = public source. Accepted for the prototype (D-07 funnel logic); revisit at store submission.
